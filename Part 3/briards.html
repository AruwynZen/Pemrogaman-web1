The briard

, or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used ad a herder and guardian of

sheep

Breed History

The briards, which is believed to have originated in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries, messenger and to search for wounded soldier because of its fine sense of hearing. Briards were used in the Firts World War almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering. Charlemagne, Napoleon, Thomas Jeffereson and Lafayette and all owned briards.
